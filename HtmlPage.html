Dear Publisher

Welcome to MagEnhance!

Let your readers discover an all new experience with Magzter’s enhancement tool, MagEnhance.

Magzter promises you only the best technology and services and we deliver this time and again. With our newest creation MagEnhance, your magazines will literally come to life!

The MagEnhance tool lets you:

  • Create videos, audio and slide shows for pages.
  • Include web links, shopping links, email addresses and page numbers.
  • Include a variety of social sharing icons.

Continue

How MagEnhance tool works:

Welcome to MagEnhance!

  • Under Manage Magazines -> search for your magazine and click Go. You will reach the Magazine Listing page.
  • On the Magazine Listing page you will find the option -> Request for MagEnhance. Click this button to enable MagEnhance features to the magazine you have selected. The MagEnhance conversion will take about 15 minutes.
  • During this time, you will notice the following alert - Processing MagEnhance conversion. Upon completion of the same, you will see the button MagEnhance - Interactive Pages.
  • Click on "MagEnhance - Interactive Pages" to go to the MagEnhance tool.

Make the most of MagEnhance today!
